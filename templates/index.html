About Us

PST is a global technology institute focused on training, mentoring, and developing young talents into world-renowned technology experts. With a focus on computing, we are helping shape a world with an ever-increasing reliance on digital infrastructure, and bridging the gap between software and peopleware. To do this, we have embarked on a journey to train as many individuals as are willing to learn, at no extra cost, equipping them with skills needed to be at the forefront of the emerging world.



Our Mission

Our ultimate goal is to impact the world using technology. We aim to show individuls, not just the power of tech, but what they themselves can achieve if they believe and work towards it. It is our firm beleif that man can achieve whatsoever his imagination can conceive. We hope to get more people to see the world like we do.
